Event Overview

The Tech Summit 2024 held on 15th March 2024 at BIT Durg was a great success. The event aimed to bring together tech enthusiasts, industry leaders, and students to discuss the future of technology and innovation.

Key Highlights:

  • Guest Speakers:
    • Dr. John Smith, CEO of Tech Innovators
    • Ms. Emily Brown, Head of Research at FutureTech
    • Mr. Robert White, CTO of TechnoCorp
  • Topics Covered:
    • Artificial Intelligence in 2024
    • Blockchain and its Impact on Society
    • The Future of Smart Cities
  • Workshops:
    • AI Workshop: Building Your First Neural Network
    • Blockchain Basics: Hands-on Workshop

Audience Engagement:

Over 300 participants from various colleges and industries attended the event. The sessions were interactive, and attendees actively engaged with the speakers during the Q&A rounds.

Photo Gallery

Feedback and Takeaways

Participants shared positive feedback, particularly highlighting the insightful discussions on AI and blockchain. The hands-on workshops were well received, providing valuable knowledge and skills for the attendees.

Future Events

The success of Tech Summit 2024 has paved the way for more events in the field of technology. We are planning to host workshops and seminars in the coming months on topics like Machine Learning and Internet of Things (IoT).
